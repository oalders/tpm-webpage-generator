PerlHandler

  • The PerlHandler stage is what most people care about
  • It is the stage that is responsible for the generation of output (usually HTML) to give to the web browser, per its request
  • Approximately, this is what a "CGI Program" would do
  • Consequentially, mod_perl itself comes bundled with two modules that provide "transparent" CGI emulation layers
    • Apache::PerlRun
    • Apache::Registry
